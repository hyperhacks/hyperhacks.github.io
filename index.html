Chapter 1: Basic Algebra

Here are some basic algebra formulas:

  • Quadratic Formula: \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)
  • Pythagorean Theorem: \( c = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \)

Chapter 2: Trigonometry

Some trigonometry formulas:

  • Sine Rule: \( \frac{\sin A}{a} = \frac{\sin B}{b} = \frac{\sin C}{c} \)
  • Cosine Rule: \( c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab \cos C \)

Chapter 3: Calculus

Some calculus formulas:

  • Derivative of a function: \( f'(x) = \lim_{{h \to 0}} \frac{{f(x+h) - f(x)}}{h} \)
  • Integral of a function: \( \int f(x) \, dx \)
